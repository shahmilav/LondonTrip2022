Buckingham Palace

The home of Her Majesty the Queen. For over 300 years before living here, Kings lived in St. James Palace. (1531-1837). The first member of the Crown to live here was Queen Victoria. The palace is about 830,000 sq. ft. large, consisting of over 700 rooms.
